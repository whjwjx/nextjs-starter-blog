### 🌙 About Me
I am **Huajiang Wang**. Rather than the vague label of "programmer," I prefer to define myself as a **Wall-Breaker in the Information World**.

The tech circle suffers from a strange "inflation disease" — wrapping simple logic in complex jargon, as if not dropping a few acronyms makes one unprofessional. But I have always believed that **profoundness does not equal obscurity; clarity is the highest form of wisdom.** What I do is strip away those layers of over-encapsulation and restore technology to its most original, unadorned skeleton.

- 🎓 **Identity**: Master's student (Class of 2027). Keeping a cool head amidst the AI fever.
- 🎯 **Vision**: Not doing tech for tech's sake, but to solve real-world problems.
- 🌐 **Territory**: [huajiang.wang](http://huajiang.wang)

### 🌿 Why Write This Blog?
In this era of information overload, adding another blog seems superfluous. But if it is to **resist mediocre repetition**, then its existence is necessary.

Here, you won't find copy-pasted tutorials, but logic distilled through thought:
- **Disenchantment of AI**: Dragging deified concepts like Agent and MCP down from the altar to see what concrete problems they actually solve.
- **The Essence of Engineering**: Code is not just a pile of functions, but an **art of fighting entropy**. From Vue to React, tools change, but the pursuit of structure and order remains constant.
- **Rational Voice**: In the noisy wave of technology, what we need is not more trending topics, but more common sense.
