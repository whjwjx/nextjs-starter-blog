### 🌙 About Me
I am **Huajiang Wang**. Rather than the rigid label of "programmer," I prefer to see myself as a **translator** wandering in the world of code.

In this technical circle full of "jargon" and anxiety, I always want to slow down. I want to tell you about those cold algorithms and complex architectures as if I were talking about everyday things. To me, **technology should not be a cold high wall, but a gentle breeze that happens to blow in after pushing open a window.**

- 🎓 **Identity**: Master's student (Class of 2027), "wandering" in the vast world of AI.
- 🎯 **Vision**: Wanting to use technology to add a bit of sugar to life, even if it's just a tiny bit of sweetness.
- 🌐 **Little Nest**: [huajiang.wang](http://huajiang.wang)

### 🌿 What is This Blog For?
This is not some serious textbook; it's more like a **technical handbook** I've tucked away, recording some trivial insights.

I will write down here:
- **Daily whispers with AI partners**: It's sometimes scary smart, and sometimes adorably clumsy. See how I grind with it and turn tedious work into a kind of enjoyment.
- **The joy of building small worlds**: From Vue to React, from back-end to front-end. Writing code is like quietly building blocks in the middle of the night; every time a piece is put together, a light turns on in my heart.
- **Whimsical ideas about the future**: Agent, MCP... what kind of gentle possibilities are hidden behind these words?
